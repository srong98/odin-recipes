Waffles

Directions

This recipe will be used to create the classic breakfast delight, waffles. With just a 25 minute preparation time, this recipe is the perfect way to start off your day and you'll even have extras for later.


Now let's get into the ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar in a large bowl
  2. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs
  4. Stir in the milk, butter, and vanilla
  5. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture
  6. Beat until blended
  7. Ladle the batter into preheated waffle iron
  8. Cook waffles until golden and crisp
