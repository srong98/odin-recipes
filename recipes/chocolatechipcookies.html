Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This recipe will be used to make the classic dessert of a chocolate chip cookie. With the ingredients listed below, by following the steps, you'll have a nice batch of chocolate chip cookies ready within an hour, freshly baked and delicious to eat.


Now let's start with the ingrdients that you'll need!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F (175° C)
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time
  4. Stir in vanilla
  5. Dissolve baking soda in hot water
  6. Add to batter along with salt
  7. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts
  8. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes or until edges are nicely browned
